Mail Merge by Samarth Verma

Directions:

1. User Email: Enter the address through which the emails will be sent

2. Gmail Key: Enter the app password (This data is not stored anywhere)

* Type in your browser "manage my google account"
* Within the search bar "App passwords"
* Name it anything and press Create

3. Parameters: Fields that will be replaced in each email

* It is recommended to distinguish the parameters with braces {}
* Example: {name}, {company}, {color}

4. Emails: The amount of emails that will be sent out

* Here you can input what will replace your parameters
* Example: John, Tesla, Blue (This can vary for each email)

5. Subject/Body: Subject and Bodies of the emails

* Use your parameters to replace text in the emails

6. Follow-up Subject/Body: Subject and Bodies of the follow-up emails

7. Choose the delay between the initial and follow-up emails
